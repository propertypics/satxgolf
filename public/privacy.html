Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 13, 2025

SaTrail.com is a local community website that helps golfers keep track of their golfing experiences. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

  • Information We Collect: We collect your name, email address, and golf activity information (tee times, courses played, scores) to provide our service.
  • How We Use Your Information: We use your information to manage your account, track your golf statistics, send notifications about tee times, and provide course updates.
  • Email Communications: If you opt in, we'll send you notifications about available tee times and important announcements. You can opt out anytime through your account settings or by clicking unsubscribe in any email.
  • Data Security: We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access or disclosure.
  • Your Rights: You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. Contact us at support@satrail.com for any privacy-related concerns.

By using SaTrail.com, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.
